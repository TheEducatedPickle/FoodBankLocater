About

In Santa Cruz County, 1 in 5 people face hunger in Santa Cruz County. For children it’s 1 in 4. Hunger is not inevitable. We’re working hard to feed people who need food now and to advocate for policies that tackle the root causes of hunger. We reach people in need through our vast network of partner agencies and non-profits and directly through food distribution and nutrition education at dozens of program sites.
